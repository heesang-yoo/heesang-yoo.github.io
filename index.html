Hee Sang Yoo, Ph.D.

I hold a Ph.D. in Nuclear Engineering from Seoul National University and currently work as a postdoctoral researcher. During my doctoral studies under Prof. Eung Soo Kim, I developed particle-based CFD codes in CUDA C++ named SOPHIA, focusing on thermal-hydraulic and safety aspects within the nuclear field. Based on these developed codes, I performed simulations on droplet dynamics, highly viscous fluid interactions, and corium spreading, and participated in various internal projects, including multi-GPU code extensions.

I also have experience with commercial CFD programs and system codes such as MODELICA and DYMOLA, and I am expanding my capabilities through various collaborative projects. These include modeling the EU-APR 1400 core catcher using particle-based code and SMR system modeling based on MODELICA. My expertise encompasses high-performance computing, multi-physics simulations, and a proactive, collaborative approach to research. I am eager to continue applying my skills and knowledge to contribute to innovative research and projects in the nuclear engineering field.

Please feel free to contact me!
